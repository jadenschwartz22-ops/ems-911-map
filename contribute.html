What makes good data

  • County-by-county: Who provides 911 ambulance transport in each county?
  • Agency type: Fire-based, private (AMR, Falck, etc.), county EMS, or hospital-based?
  • Contract details: Is it exclusive? When does the contract expire?
  • Boundaries: Do you have GIS/shapefile data for fire or EMS districts?
  • Nuance: Who does first response vs. transport? Any dual-provider areas?

Data Sources We Use

  • State EMS licensing registries (CDPHE, GA DPH, etc.)
  • Fire protection district GIS boundaries (DOLA, Census TIGER)
  • RETAC / Regional EMS council provider lists
  • Agency websites and job postings
  • Municipal contract records
  • Community knowledge from EMS professionals

This is an open community project. All data is public information compiled from official sources. No patient data, no HIPAA concerns - just who covers what.
